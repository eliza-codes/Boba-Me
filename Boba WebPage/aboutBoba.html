About Boba


Thank you for choosing Boba Me to get your facts about your favorite drink, boba. This article has been written in 2019 so some facts might not be up to date.

Your favorite drink is often referred to as boba; however, boba is actually the small tapioca pearls that are included with your drink. Boba is often a pleasant chewy delight when cooked. It may be slightly sweet depending on which sweeteners the pearl has been made with. Your drink can have different add-ons. They can have a popping feeling.
